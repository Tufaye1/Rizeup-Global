Questions? Answered.

The eight things every student (and every parent) asks us first.

The first consult is completely free. No card, no commitment. If you sign on, we charge one flat package fee that covers all six services. No per-document surprises, and we tell you the exact number before you pay anything.
Malaysia, by a wide margin. Tuition starts around $3,500/yr and living costs are close to Dhaka. Europe (especially Germany and Italy) can be cheaper on tuition but pricier to live in. We map real total costs against your budget in the first consult.
Less than you think. Most Malaysian universities accept a 3.5+ HSC GPA; Australia and the USA look at your whole profile: grades, English score, SOP. Weak in one area? We show you which universities weigh the areas where you're strong.
For Australia, the USA, and most of Europe, yes. For Malaysia, many universities accept MOI letters or their own English placement tests. If you do need IELTS, we tell you the exact band your target universities require, not a guess.
We can't promise one. Anyone who promises is lying. What we do: match your profile against scholarships you're realistically eligible for and build those applications properly. 40%+ tuition waivers are common in Malaysia for strong HSC results.
Rejections almost always trace to documentation gaps or a shaky interview, the two things we control hardest. We prepare your file the way embassies want it and run mock interviews until you're calm. If a refusal still happens, we handle the reapplication.
Malaysia: as fast as 2–3 months from first consult to flight. Australia and the USA: plan for 6–9 months including visa. The single biggest delay is starting late. If you finished HSC this year, talk to us now, not after results season.
Please bring them. Half our consults include a parent on the call, Bangla or English, whichever they prefer. They can visit the Chattogram office in person, and parents of students in Malaysia can reach our Shah Alam office directly.
